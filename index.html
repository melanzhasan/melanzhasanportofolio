Customer Behavior Analysis using Python

This project is about analysis of customer behavior and was inspired by this project. This analysis is essential for any company seeking to understand its ideal customer base. This process not only enhances a business's comprehension of its clientele but also streamlines the customization of products to align with the distinct needs, behaviors, and concerns of various customer segments. The dataset has collected on crucial aspects such as educational level, marital status, year of birth, expenditure on our products, and purchase modes over the past two years. The focus of our investigation lies in unraveling the factors influencing customer spending patterns.
